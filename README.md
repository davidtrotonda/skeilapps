# SkeilApps

SkeilApps is an ecommerce app agency focused on helping online stores launch their own mobile apps for iOS and Android.

The goal is simple: turn an ecommerce store into a mobile-first sales and retention channel, so brands can increase repeat purchases, improve customer access and build a stronger direct relationship with their audience.

[Visit the website](https://skeilapps.com/)

![SkeilApps website homepage](./assets/01-website-homepage.jpeg)

## What SkeilApps does

SkeilApps creates mobile app experiences for ecommerce businesses that already sell online and want a more direct channel with their customers.

The service is built around practical business outcomes:

- Mobile apps for ecommerce brands.
- iOS and Android app presence.
- Mobile-first shopping experience.
- Retention and repeat purchase channel.
- App Store and Google Play publishing support.
- Ecommerce-focused product strategy.

## Why it exists

Many ecommerce businesses depend on ads, marketplaces, email and web traffic. Those channels are useful, but they do not always create a direct habit with customers.

A mobile app can become a dedicated channel for customers who already know the brand, especially when the ecommerce has:

- Repeat purchase potential.
- A catalog that benefits from browsing.
- Loyal customers.
- Seasonal launches or product drops.
- A reason to communicate directly through app-based flows.
